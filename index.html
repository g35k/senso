What is SENSO?

SENSO is an affordable, compact braille learning tool designed for kids and beginners. Learn up to grade two braille with our teacher, Sen.

Six tactile dot buttons map directly to a braille cell — your fingers learn the language naturally, just by playing.

Our Mission

Breaking barriers through Braille literacy

Blindness affects over 43 million people worldwide. Braille literacy opens doors to education, independence, and opportunity — yet most learners never get access to proper tools.
